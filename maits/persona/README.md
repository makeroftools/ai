Persona is a flexible and secure identity verification (KYC/KYB) platform used
to verify real people and real businesses online. The WeOwn ecosystem plans to
integrate Persona into its payment and onboarding flows, including a 24-hour
KYC trigger for payment release and invite-only contributor onboarding.

Persona provides modular building blocks:
- **Verifications** — Identity checks with modern signals and authoritative records
- **Dynamic Flow** — Custom information-collection flows that maximize conversion
- **Workflows** — Automated identity processes around KYC, KYB, and verification
- **Graph** — Coordinated fraud detection via link analysis
- **Cases** — Human-in-the-loop review and investigation
- **Relay** — Privacy-first data sharing that keeps consumer data within Persona

Prototype stage — awaiting PRJ-401 scope and access direction.
